Lost Sword Basic Team Building
Meta 12.03.2025

Overview

  • The meta is divided into two parts.
  • "Galahad" to give shooters and wizards a powerful buff
  • "Ran card" that deals counterattack damage in proportion to your attack.
  • Once you have secured one or both of these, it is recommended that you have a variety of healers to increase your deck's flexibility.
  • Securing the main dealers is also important, but from a long-term perspective, it is advantageous to meet the above first.

Resonance System & Cards

What is Resonance?

The game features a unique Resonance System that allows players to exchange duplicate heroes for Stats or Resonance Cards.

Card Features

These special cards have distinct characteristics:

  • No class or attribute restrictions
  • Can dramatically alter gameplay (meta-changing)

Tip

Before using duplicate heroes for Transcendence, prioritize looking for Meta and creating cards. The strategic advantages of cards provide long-term value over immediate stat boosts.

Transcendence Mechanics

Core Benefits

  • Base stat increases:
    • Damage: +X%

Progression Milestones

Transcendence Level Benefits
Level 3
  • All skill levels +1
  • Stat boost increase
Level 5 (Awakening)
  • All skill levels +1
  • Chance for new skills
  • Maximum stat boost

Optimization Strategy

Prioritize reaching levels 3 and 5:
The skill enhancements at these thresholds provide more value than linear stat increases. Plan your hero duplicates accordingly:

  • Total duplicates needed for full power: 8
  • Recommended progression path: 3 → 5 → Awakening

Team Building

Core Roles

1. Main DPS: Main Attack Damage
2. Healer: Buffs, debuffs, and healing to enhance your team's performance.
3. Tank/Support: Absorbs damage , protects / buff your team.
4. Second DPS: Secondary Attack Dealer to balance your team's damage output.

Team Synergy

1. Buffs & Debuffs: Coordinate skills that enhance each other.
2. Healing & Sustain: Ensure your team can survive longer battles.

Meta Considerations

1. Current Meta: Check the latest tier lists and updates for optimal team compositions.
2. Flexibility: Adapt your team based on the game mode and opponent.
