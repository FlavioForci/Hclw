Requirements for unlocking Unique Equipment

Clear stage 5-1

Here are the stages where Unique Equipment items start to drop!

  • Unique Equipment items do not drop while you are offline! They have a small chance to drop while you are online!
  • Common Unique Equipment Chest: from 5-1
  • Common-Uncommon Unique Equipment Chest: from 28-1
  • Common-Rare Unique Equipment Chest: from 43-1
  • Common-Magic Unique Equipment Chest: from 53-1

How do I check my offline rewards?

From the main screen, enter the Stage menu! You can check the rewards in the Offline Reward menu at the top-right corner!

When is the weekly reset?

Every Wednesday, at 12:00 p.m. noon (UTC+9)

Game accounts already linked to DOSI

Accounts already linked to DOSI cannot be unlinked or changed as per DOSI’s policies! Please choose the account you want to link carefully!

How to claim items purchased from DOSI:

  • After payment, go to My Assets (Items) to check the purchased item.
  • Enter the game and request to claim the purchased item via Start Trading.
  • The item will arrive in your inventory as a box, which you can then open to claim it.

Skill

Skill Scroll can be gained from chest dropped in-stage or can be bought from marketplace. Make sure that the skill is on Auto Mode.

Tips: Build skill based on what you have for starter. You can experiment skill combo after you gain more skills.

Accessory Damage Bonus

There are 3 types of damage bonus that you can gain:

  • DMG to Normal Monster Amplification: Damage multiplier that only applies to normal monsters.
  • DMG to Boss Monster Amplification: Damage multiplier that only applies to boss monsters.
  • Final DMG Amplification: Final damage multiplier applies to all DMG.

Tips: Focus on DMG to Boss Monster Amplification, then Final Damage Amplification as you will most likely get stuck when fighting a boss.
