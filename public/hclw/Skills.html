Skill Guide

Elements and Their Effects

  • Physical Element:
    Debuffs - Bleed, Stasis, Knockback;
    Buffs - Increased Damage, Attack Speed, HP Recovery
  • Lightning Element:
    Debuffs - Electrocute, Stuns, AOE;
    Buffs - Increased Smite Rate, HP Recovery, Increased Damage
  • Flame Element:
    Debuffs - Burn, Knockback;
    Buffs - Increased Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Attack Speed, HP Recovery
  • Ice Element:
    Debuffs - Freeze, CC;
    Buffs - Increased Damage, Mana Recovery, Defense Down
  • Earth Element:
    Debuffs - Restrain, Stun;
    Buffs - Increased Basic Attack, HP Recovery, Increased Damage
  • Divine Element:
    Debuffs - Stun, Defense Down;
    Buffs - Increased Skill Damage, HP and Mana Regen, Damage to Stunned
  • Void Element:
    Debuffs - Infected DoT, Fear, Attack Speed Down;
    Buffs - Damage Amplification, Increased Movement Speed
  • Summon Element:
    Debuffs - Taunt, Attack Speed Down;
    Buffs - Increased Damage per Summon, Increased Summon HP
